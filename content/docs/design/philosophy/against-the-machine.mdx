### Johannes speaking

Arkitekt is a platform of the pre-ai area and was mainly written and conceptualized before the AI boom. While
auto-complete was a loveable addition to our design and development workflow and arguably Arkitekt would have lasted a year longer
if it wasnt around, the recent AI agentic workflows leave a bitter taste in my mouths.

Don't get us wrong, AI *could*  be a great addition to the scientific process, but currently i am even fighting
my editor in writing this text, **as it keeps trying to "improve" it**. I cannot even
write a personal note without being harressd by a tool. But i don't care about writing text, I cared about
writing code.
