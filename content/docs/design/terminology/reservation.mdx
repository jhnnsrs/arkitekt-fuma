### What is a Reservation

A reservation is a booking of a specific Node on Arkitekt. It represents a contract between
as user (and its app) and arkitekt for the usage of some bioimage task (e.g a segmentation).

Arkitekt tries to uphold the reservation, by adding consuming provisions on the other end.


## Reservation as Topic 

A reservation in Arkitekt is closely mimic the concepot of a topic in RabbitMQ. 
It is a message queue that can be consumed by multiple consumers.
